XIUM – Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users can request deletion of their account and associated data from the XIUM app.

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the XIUM app
  • Go to your Profile or Settings
  • Tap on Delete Account
  • Confirm your action

Data Deletion

When you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.

  • Profile information (name, email)
  • Uploaded documents (invoices, receipts, warranties)
  • Any other user-related data stored in the app

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. All data is permanently deleted.

Contact

If you have any issues deleting your account, please contact at contact@xium.io.
